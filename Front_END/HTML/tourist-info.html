Discover Wines

Red Wines

Made from grapes with skins that release tannins during fermentation. Popular red wine varietals include Cabernet Sauvignon, Zinfandel, Merlot, Pinot Noir, and more.

White Wines

Produced with little or no skin contact, resulting in pink or white wines with fewer tannins. Popular white wine varietals include Chardonnay, Riesling, Sauvignon Blanc, and others.

Rosé Wines

Pink in color, obtained by shorter contact with red grape skins. Contains lower tannin levels compared to red wines. Popular rosé varietals include Pinot Noir, Zinfandel, Pinot Grigio, and Sangiovese.

Dessert Wines

Sweet wines consumed after a meal. Popular dessert wines include Port, Madeira, Vermouth, Sherry, and Marsala.

Sparkling Wines

Carbonated wine obtained through natural fermentation or carbon dioxide injection. Popular sparkling wines include Champagne, Prosecco, Cava, Sparkling Rosé, Moscato, and Lambrusco.
